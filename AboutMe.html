My Mission:

My mission is to work on my technical skills in the web development field as a professional freelancer here
in Guatemala and gain experience too. I also want to demonstrate potential to take on new responsibilities
and challenges, always learn new material within my career and satisfy my clients with the requirements that
they want on their future websites. In addition in the future I want to turn this freelance work into a web
development and design company.

My Vision:

My vision is to always work hard on what I do and achieve my goals either in the short or long run. I view my
career as dynamic, fun and diverse where I can develop different types of websites for different clients. I
see the people I work with as individuals I can help and offer my services to fulfill their needs.

My Experience:

I attended the University of Louisiana at Lafayette where I got my Bachelor of Science in the Informatics field with a
concentration in Interactive Media Technology. Informatics is the study of information science or computational systems,
processing data and analyzing it for storage and retrieval. Interactive Media is similar to Graphic Design and it involves
the use of graphics, text, moving image, animation, video and audio for websites, gaming, social media and among others.

On the other hand, I have very little work experience it is compensated with my enthusiasm, desire to learn, and with
the clear objective that I am looking for a future within the web development concentration. I have worked in 3 different
companies here in Guatemala: United Nations for the development in Guatemala, Conduent and EnerGuate: Light for my Land. In
the United Nations I was in a position called Support Assistant in Website Updates, e-newsletter, and Social Networks. I was
in charged of updating the UN website in Guatemala through the use of Wordpress, I also updated the Social Media for this
organization. I was there for about 2 and a half months because it was an internship.
